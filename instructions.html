How to setup your personal vault

Connect your wallet

Click “Connect Wallet” to link your crypto wallet with CroVault.

For enhanced privacy and safety, we recommend creating a dedicated wallet address for CroVault use only — containing no assets other than a small amount of CRO for transaction fees.

If your wallet doesn’t appear after clicking “Connect Wallet” — especially on mobile devices — try opening crovault.com directly within your wallet app’s built-in browser.
