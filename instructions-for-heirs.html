Instructions for Heirs

If you are reading this, it means you have been entrusted to access a CroVault vault after the original owner's passing. Please follow the steps below carefully to retrieve and secure the stored data.

1. What You Will Need

  • The original wallet’s Seed Phrase (12 or 24 secret words).
  • The Vault Password set by the original owner.
  • Access to a compatible Web3 wallet (such as MetaMask).
  • A small amount of CRO tokens in the wallet to pay transaction fees if necessary.

2. Step-by-Step Access Guide

  1. Recover the Wallet: Use the provided Seed Phrase to restore the wallet in a Web3 application like MetaMask. Important: Do this on a secure and trusted device.
  2. Connect to CroVault: Visit crovault.com/vault and connect the recovered wallet.
  3. Unlock the Vault: Enter the Vault Password when prompted to unlock, sign the message in the wallet and decrypt the stored data.
  4. Review and Save Data: Once unlocked, you can view credentials, notes, wallet addresses, PINs, bank accounts, insurance policies, identity documents, legal documents, assets, emergency contacts, and subscription records.
  5. Secure the Data: Export or securely back up any critical information as needed. Remember: the vault will remain on the blockchain, but only you now control it.

3. Important Warnings

  • Never share the Seed Phrase or Vault Password with anyone else unless absolutely necessary.
  • Be cautious of phishing websites — always verify you are on crovault.com
  • Act responsibly — you have been trusted with sensitive and possibly legally significant data.

4. Need Help?

CroVault is a decentralized application. We cannot access or recover any vaults. If you encounter technical issues, seek help only from trusted security professionals.
